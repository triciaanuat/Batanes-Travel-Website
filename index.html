The Batanes group of islands is located at the northernmost tip of Philippine territory.

Basco Lighthouse
Standing at 66 feet, the Basco Lighthouse towers over the island of Batan and is one of the three main lighthouses in the province of Batanes.
It has also served as a guide for the local fisherfolk since it was constructed in 2003.
